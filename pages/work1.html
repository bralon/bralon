BIO

I am Bralon (/ 'brel…ôn /), Indonesian graphic designer based in Singapore; currently pursuing Diploma in Design Communication at LASALLE College of the Arts. I aim to become a versatile and flexible visual communication designer. My practices range from collateral design, digital imaging, branding, web, publication, packaging, illustration, typography, and photography. I do not limit myself to a defined style and always look out for different possibilities.
